When did these conflicts happen?

When we observe the graph that displays the number of conflicts per year, we observe several peaks. The peaks are in 1946 (this corresponds to the first Indochina war), 1948 (which corresponds to the first Arab-Israeli war and the conflict for the independence of Burma). The last peak we notice is in 2015 and it corresponds to the beginning of several conflicts involving IS.

When looking at the number of conflicts that benefited from at least one peace agreement, we notice that most of them began between the 1960s and the 1990s.
